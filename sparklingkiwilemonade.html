Drink

Posted on 17, Feb 2018

lemonade

sparkling kiwi lemonade

Inakshi

So yummy! Deliciously refreshing in this Summer...

INGREDIENTS :-

  • Kiwi (peeled) [3 large]
  • Lemon Juice [1/4 cup]
  • Fresh Mint Leaves [10-15]
  • Sugar (depending on the ripeness/sweetness of your kiwis, may need more sugar) [1/4 cup]
  • Carbonated Water or Plain Water (chilled) [1/4 cup]
  • Crushed Ice [2 scoops]
  • A pinch of salt
Course - Drinks, Float // Prepare time - 5 mins. // yield - 1

HOW TO MAKE THIS DRINK :-

1. Add all ingredients except ice and corbonated water to a blender and blend until combined. Keep in refrigerator.

2. Just before serving, add this into a chilled glass.

3. Slowly add the chilled carbonated water and crushed ice. Stir.

4. Garnish with kiwi slices if desired.
